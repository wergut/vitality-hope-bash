About the Event

Place of Hope’s 18th Annual Lexus Hope Bash Palm Beach is Palm Beach County’s premier fundraiser raising critical funds for local foster children, homeless youth, families in transition and human trafficking survivors. Known to be one of the most exhilarating and eye-opening charitable evenings, this gala offers attendees a cocktail reception, silent auction and raffles, dinner and dancing, a live auction and more! For the fourth year in a row, Lexus Hope Bash Palm Beach will be at Trump National Jupiter.

Place of Hope is South Florida’s largest and most diverse faith-based, state licensed children and families organization, serving thousands annually, focused on providing programs and services to end the cycles of abuse, neglect, homelessness and human trafficking. We offer support to children, youth and families in need. Our goal is to help those we serve find healing and restoration, which leads to a successful future.
